FACILITY NAME: North Campus Training Center
LOCATION: 123 Innovation Drive, Seattle
TOTAL SIZE: 60,000 square feet
MANAGER: Sarah Jenkins

GYM INFORMATION:
The gym is located on the second floor. It contains 20 treadmills and a full weight set. 
Members must scan their ID badge to enter.

CINEMA INFORMATION:
The cinema is in the basement, Room B1. It seats 50 people. 
Movies are shown every Friday at 7:00 PM.

OTHER RULES:
No smoking is allowed anywhere on the premises. 
Pets are only allowed in the outdoor courtyard area.
